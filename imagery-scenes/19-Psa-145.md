# Psalm 145
1: תְּהִלָּ֗ה לְדָ֫וִ֥ד אֲרוֹמִמְךָ֣ אֱלוֹהַ֣י הַמֶּ֑לֶךְ וַאֲבָרֲכָ֥ה שִׁ֝מְךָ֗ לְעוֹלָ֥ם וָעֶֽד׃
2: בְּכָל־ י֥וֹם אֲבָרֲכֶ֑ךָּ וַאֲהַלְלָ֥ה שִׁ֝מְךָ֗ לְעוֹלָ֥ם וָעֶֽד׃
3: גָּ֘ד֤וֹל יְהוָ֣ה וּמְהֻלָּ֣ל מְאֹ֑ד וְ֝לִגְדֻלָּת֗וֹ אֵ֣ין חֵֽקֶר׃
4: דּ֣וֹר לְ֭דוֹר יְשַׁבַּ֣ח מַעֲשֶׂ֑יךָ וּגְב֖וּרֹתֶ֣יךָ יַגִּֽידוּ׃
5: הֲ֭דַר כְּב֣וֹד הוֹדֶ֑ךָ וְדִבְרֵ֖י נִפְלְאוֹתֶ֣יךָ אָשִֽׂיחָה׃
6: וֶעֱז֣וּז נוֹרְאֹתֶ֣יךָ יֹאמֵ֑רוּ אֲסַפְּרֶֽנָּה׃
7: זֵ֣כֶר רַב־ טוּבְךָ֣ יַבִּ֑יעוּ וְצִדְקָתְךָ֥ יְרַנֵּֽנוּ׃
8: חַנּ֣וּן וְרַח֣וּם יְהוָ֑ה אֶ֥רֶךְ אַ֝פַּ֗יִם וּגְדָל־ חָֽסֶד׃
9: טוֹב־ יְהוָ֥ה לַכֹּ֑ל וְ֝רַחֲמָ֗יו עַל־ כָּל־ מַעֲשָֽׂיו׃
10: יוֹד֣וּךָ יְ֭הוָה כָּל־ מַעֲשֶׂ֑יךָ וַ֝חֲסִידֶ֗יךָ יְבָרֲכֽוּכָה׃
11: כְּב֣וֹד מַלְכוּתְךָ֣ יֹאמֵ֑רוּ וּגְבוּרָתְךָ֥ יְדַבֵּֽרוּ׃
12: לְהוֹדִ֤יעַ ׀ לִבְנֵ֣י הָ֭אָדָם גְּבוּרֹתָ֑יו וּ֝כְב֗וֹד הֲדַ֣ר מַלְכוּתֽוֹ׃
13: מַֽלְכוּתְךָ֗ מַלְכ֥וּת כָּל־ עֹֽלָמִ֑ים וּ֝מֶֽמְשֶׁלְתְּךָ֗ בְּכָל־ דּ֥וֹר וָדֽוֹר׃
14: סוֹמֵ֣ךְ יְ֭הוָה לְכָל־ הַנֹּפְלִ֑ים וְ֝זוֹקֵ֗ף לְכָל־ הַכְּפוּפִֽים׃
15: עֵֽינֵי־ כֹ֭ל אֵלֶ֣יךָ יְשַׂבֵּ֑רוּ וְאַתָּ֤ה נֽוֹתֵן־ לָהֶ֖ם אֶת־ אָכְלָ֣ם בְּעִתּֽוֹ׃
16: פּוֹתֵ֥חַ אֶת־ יָדֶ֑ךָ וּמַשְׂבִּ֖יעַ לְכָל־ חַ֣י רָצֽוֹן׃
17: צַדִּ֣יק יְ֭הוָה בְּכָל־ דְּרָכָ֑יו וְ֝חָסִ֗יד בְּכָל־ מַעֲשָֽׂיו׃
18: קָר֣וֹב יְ֭הוָה לְכָל־ קֹרְאָ֑יו לְכֹ֤ל אֲשֶׁ֖ר יִקְרָאֻ֣הוּ בֶאֱמֶֽת׃
19: רְצוֹן־ יְרֵאָ֥יו יַעֲשֶׂ֑ה וְֽאֶת־ שַׁוְעָתָ֥ם יִ֝שְׁמַ֗ע וְיוֹשִׁיעֵֽם׃
20: שׁוֹמֵ֣ר יְ֭הוָה אֶת־ כָּל־ אֹהֲבָ֑יו וְאֵ֖ת כָּל־ הָרְשָׁעִ֣ים יַשְׁמִֽיד׃
21: תְּהִלַּ֥ת יְהוָ֗ה יְֽדַבֶּ֫ר־ פִּ֥י וִיבָרֵ֣ךְ כָּל־ בָּ֭שָׂר שֵׁ֥ם קָדְשׁ֗וֹ לְעוֹלָ֥ם וָעֶֽד׃

# Imagery Outline of Psalm 145

## Scene 1: The Greatness of God

> "I will exalt you, my God the King; I will praise your name for ever and ever. Every day I will praise you and extol your name for ever and ever." - Psalm 145:1-2 (WLC)

- Scene:
  - A believer exalting and praising God.
- Imagery:
  - The believer lifting their voice in praise and exaltation.
  - The believer expressing their devotion and admiration for God.
  - The believer committing to praising God every day.

## Scene 2: God's Splendor and Greatness

> "Great is the LORD and most worthy of praise; his greatness no one can fathom." - Psalm 145:3 (WLC)

- Scene:
  - The vastness of God's greatness beyond human comprehension.
- Imagery:
  - God's greatness extending beyond human understanding.
  - The splendor and majesty of God's being.
  - The immeasurable value of praising and worshiping God.

## Scene 3: God's Works and Mighty Deeds

> "One generation commends your works to another; they tell of your mighty acts." - Psalm 145:4 (WLC)

- Scene:
  - The passing down of testimonies of God's mighty acts from one generation to another.
- Imagery:
  - Believers sharing stories and testimonies of God's miraculous works.
  - The transmission of faith and the passing down of God's deeds throughout generations.
  - The importance of preserving and sharing the knowledge of God's powerful acts.

## Scene 4: God's Glorious and Wonderful Works

> "They speak of the glorious splendor of your majesty -- and I will meditate on your wonderful works." - Psalm 145:5 (WLC)

- Scene:
  - Contemplating and reflecting on God's glorious and wonderful works.
- Imagery:
  - The believer lost in thought, meditating on the magnificence of God's majesty.
  - The believer contemplating the incredible nature of God's works.
  - The act of intentionally focusing on God's marvelous deeds and finding awe and wonder in them.

## Scene 5: God's Greatness and Compassion

> "They tell of the power of your awesome works -- and I will proclaim your great deeds." - Psalm 145:6 (WLC)

- Scene:
  - Proclaiming God's great deeds and the power of His awesome works.
- Imagery:
  - Believers declaring the impact and significance of God's awe-inspiring acts.
  - The believer expressing gratitude for God's incredible power.
  - The act of testifying and proclaiming God's glorious deeds to others.

## Scene 6: God's Abundant Goodness

> "They celebrate your abundant goodness and joyfully sing of your righteousness." - Psalm 145:7 (WLC)

- Scene:
  - Celebrating God's abundant goodness and singing joyfully of His righteousness.
- Imagery:
  - The believer filled with joy, celebrating and rejoicing in God's overflowing goodness.
  - The act of singing with joy and gratitude for God's righteousness.
  - The believer expressing delight and jubilation in the recognition of God's character.

## Scene 7: God's Compassion and Love

> "The LORD is gracious and compassionate, slow to anger and rich in love." - Psalm 145:8 (WLC)

- Scene:
  - Describing God's graciousness, compassion, patience, and love.
- Imagery:
  - The believer experiencing and acknowledging God's graciousness and compassion.
  - The believer recognizing God's patience and slow anger.
  - The abundant and overflowing nature of God's love towards His creation.

## Scene 8: God's Goodness and Care for All

> "The LORD is good to all; he has compassion on all he has made." - Psalm 145:9 (WLC)

- Scene:
  - God's goodness and compassion extending to all of His creation.
- Imagery:
  - God's care and provision for every living being.
  - The believer witnessing God's goodness in the world.
  - The comprehensive and inclusive nature of God's compassion.

## Scene 9: All Creation Praising God

> "All your works praise you, LORD; your faithful people extol you." - Psalm 145:10 (WLC)

- Scene:
  - All of God's creation offering praise to Him.
- Imagery:
  - The believer perceiving the praise of God's works in the world.
  - The believer joining in the chorus of praise and exaltation.
  - The harmony and unity of all creation glorifying God.

## Scene 10: God's Kingdom and Reign

> "They tell of the glory of your kingdom and speak of your might, so that all people may know of your mighty acts and the glorious splendor of your kingdom." - Psalm 145:11-12 (WLC)

- Scene:
  - Declaring the glory, might, and splendor of God's kingdom.
- Imagery:
  - The believer proclaiming the majesty and dominion of God's kingdom.
  - The spreading of the knowledge of God's mighty acts and the magnificence of His reign.
  - The desire for all people to experience and be aware of God's sovereign rule.

## Scene 11: God's Faithfulness and Compassion

> "Your kingdom is an everlasting kingdom, and your dominion endures through all generations. The LORD is trustworthy in all he promises and faithful in all he does." - Psalm 145:13 (WLC)

- Scene:
  - God's eternal and unwavering faithfulness and trustworthiness.
- Imagery:
  - The believer acknowledging God's kingdom as everlasting and enduring throughout generations.
  - The expression of confidence and reliance on God's promises.
  - The believer recognizing God's faithfulness in all His actions.

## Scene 12: God's Nearness and Deliverance

> "The LORD upholds all who fall and lifts up all who are bowed down. The eyes of all look to you, and you give them their food at the proper time. You open your hand and satisfy the desires of every living thing." - Psalm 145:14-16 (WLC)

- Scene:
  - God's nearness and deliverance to those in need.
- Imagery:
  - The believer being supported and lifted up by God when they stumble and fall.
  - The eyes of all creatures turning to God in anticipation and reliance.
  - The provision of sustenance and satisfaction by God to every living thing.

## Scene 13: God's Righteousness and Nearness

> "The LORD is righteous in all his ways and faithful in all he does. The LORD is near to all who call on him, to all who call on him in truth." - Psalm 145:17-18 (WLC)

- Scene:
  - God's righteousness, faithfulness, and nearness to those who seek Him.
- Imagery:
  - The believer recognizing God's unwavering integrity in all His ways.
  - The assurance of God's faithfulness and reliability.
  - The believer experiencing the nearness and closeness of God through sincere and genuine pursuit.

## Scene 14: God's Fulfillment of Desires

> "He fulfills the desires of those who fear him; he hears their cry and saves them." - Psalm 145:19 (WLC)

- Scene:
  - God's fulfillment of the desires of those who reverence Him.
- Imagery:
  - The believer's desires being met and satisfied through their fear and reverence for God.
  - The believer experiencing the assurance that God listens and saves in times of distress.
  - The act of crying out to God and receiving His deliverance.

## Scene 15: God's Preservation of the Faithful

> "The LORD watches over all who love him, but all the wicked he will destroy." - Psalm 145:20 (WLC)

- Scene:
  - God's watchful care and protection over those who love Him.
- Imagery:
  - God's vigilant eye watching over and safeguarding those who hold deep affection for Him.
  - God's justice and judgment against the wicked.
  - The believer finding security and reassurance in God's faithful guardianship.

## Summary

The scenes in this Psalm depict a believer exalting and praising God for His greatness, splendor, and wonderful works. They testify to God's abundant goodness, compassion, and love, while celebrating His righteousness and faithfulness. The imagery emphasizes the comprehensive nature of God's care and provision, the unity of all creation praising Him, and the eternal nature of His kingdom. The scenes also convey the believer's reliance on God's nearness, deliverance, and fulfillment of desires, along with His preservation and protection of the faithful. Overall, the Psalm paints a vivid picture of God's attributes and invites believers to join in the worship and proclamation of His greatness.
