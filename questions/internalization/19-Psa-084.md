# Psalm 84

Psalm 84 is a beautiful psalm that expresses longing for God and the joy of dwelling in His presence. Here are some discussion questions organized by sections of the psalm to help people relate it to their own cultural setting:

Verses 1-4:

1. How does the psalmist describe their longing for God's dwelling place? How does this longing resonate with your own cultural understanding of seeking and yearning for God?

2. In what ways do the birds finding a home in the Lord's house and the sparrow finding a nest for herself reflect the psalmist's desire to be in God's presence? How can we find and create spaces for ourselves to experience God's presence in our own cultural context?

Verses 5-7:

1. As the psalmist journeys towards God's dwelling place, they experience strength along the way. How does this idea of finding strength on the journey resonate with your own cultural beliefs about perseverance and faith in times of difficulty?

2. The psalmist describes the journey to God's dwelling place as passing through the Valley of Baca, a place of weeping. How does the concept of finding springs in the midst of a dry and weeping place resonate with your cultural beliefs about finding comfort and solace in difficult times?

Verses 8-9:

1. The psalmist addresses God directly and expresses a desire to behold His face. How does this desire to see and experience God's presence resonate with your own cultural beliefs about encountering the divine?

2. The psalmist describes God as a shield. How does this imagery of God protecting and shielding His people resonate with your cultural understanding of God's care and provision?

Verses 10-12:

1. The psalmist expresses a preference to be a doorkeeper in the house of God rather than dwell in the tents of wickedness. How does this preference reflect the psalmist's values and priorities? How can we prioritize our relationship with God in our own cultural context, despite external temptations and distractions?

2. The psalm ends with a blessing for those who trust in God. How does this blessing resonate with your cultural beliefs about the rewards and blessings of faith and trust in God?

Overall Questions:

1. How does this psalm inspire you to seek after God and desire His presence? Are there any particular words, phrases, or ideas that you find especially meaningful or relatable?

2. In what ways can you apply the themes of longing for God's presence and finding strength in the journey to your own daily life and relationship with God, considering your own cultural context?

3. How does this psalm speak to the idea of finding solace and strength in God's presence amidst challenging circumstances? How can we encourage and support one another in finding comfort and renewal in God in our own cultural setting?

Feel free to tailor these questions to your specific cultural context and add any additional questions that come to mind while discussing the psalm.
